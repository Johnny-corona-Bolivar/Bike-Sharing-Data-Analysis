# 🏢 Business Understanding

## Company Background

Cyclistic launched its bike-sharing service in **2016** and has grown into one of Chicago's largest bicycle-sharing systems.

The company currently operates:

- 🚲 **5,824 bicycles**
- 📍 **692 docking stations**
- 🌎 Coverage throughout Chicago

Cyclistic offers several bicycle types, including:

- Classic Bikes
- Electric Bikes
- Cargo Bikes
- Hand Tricycles
- Recumbent Bikes

Unlike many bike-sharing services, Cyclistic focuses on accessibility by providing bicycles designed for users with different mobility needs.

Customers can unlock a bicycle at any station and return it to any other station within the network.

---

## Pricing Plans

Cyclistic currently offers three pricing options:

| Plan | Customer Type |
|-------|---------------|
| Single Ride Pass | Casual Rider |
| Full-Day Pass | Casual Rider |
| Annual Membership | Member |

Financial analysis has shown that **Annual Members generate considerably higher long-term profitability than Casual Riders**.

Instead of investing exclusively in attracting new customers, Cyclistic's marketing team believes that converting existing casual riders into annual members represents the greatest opportunity for sustainable business growth.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Cyclistic wants to understand how riding behavior differs between **Casual Riders** and **Annual Members**.

By identifying these behavioral differences, the Marketing Team can develop targeted campaigns that encourage casual riders to purchase annual memberships, increasing profitability while strengthening long-term customer retention.

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

Analyze twelve months of historical trip data to identify customer behavior patterns that explain how annual members and casual riders use Cyclistic bicycles differently.

The results of this analysis will support data-driven marketing strategies aimed at increasing annual memberships and promoting sustainable business growth.

# ❓ Business Questions

The analysis is driven by three key business questions:

1. How do annual members and casual riders use Cyclistic bikes differently?

2. Why would casual riders purchase an annual membership?

3. How can digital media influence casual riders to become annual members?

This project focuses on answering the **first business question**, providing data-driven insights that support future marketing decisions.

# ⚠️ Data Limitations

Although the dataset is suitable for identifying customer behavior patterns, several limitations should be considered:

- Personal customer information is unavailable due to privacy regulations.
- Demographic variables such as age, gender, occupation, and income are not included.
- Weather conditions, holidays, and public events are not available.
- Customer motivations cannot be inferred directly from trip records.
- Findings are based on historical data and may not fully represent future customer behavior.
